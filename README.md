PROBLEMS TO OVERCOME IN GAME

* Drill heat
    * Have to wait to let it cool down, drill isnt functional when overheating
    * Buy cooling to increase time between over heating
    * No manual intervention required to stop overheating, just wait
* Drill bits break
    * Take a while to break
    * Once broken require manual intervention to hold/play minigame to fix
    * Can get upgrades to increase time between breaks
    * Can get workers to fix
* Storage
    * When storage is full, everything stops working
    * Have to sell to get things to work
* Oil pressure
    * Pressure increases while actively spinning the wheel
    * Higher pressure increases oil production and resource discovery rates
    * Pressure slowly decreases when the rig is left idle
    * Low pressure reduces drilling efficiency but does not remove resources already collected
    * Can be upgraded later with better pumps, motors, or pressure systems to slow pressure loss
    * Automated rigs maintain a portion of their pressure automatically
